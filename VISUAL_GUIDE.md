## 7. Color Scheme

### Main Colors Used:
- **WhatsApp Green**: `#25D366` - Primary buttons, active states
- **Dark Green**: `#128C7E` / `#075E54` - Headers, gradients
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Backgrounds, text on dark
- **Gray Scale**: Various grays for borders, inactive states
- **Blue**: `#3B82F6` - Intent badges, information
- **Green**: `#10B981` - Success, high confidence (>70%)
- **Yellow**: `#F59E0B` - Warning, medium confidence (50-70%)
- **Red**: `#EF4444` - Error, low confidence (<50%)

### UI Elements:
- **Buttons**: Rounded corners (rounded-lg)
- **Cards**: White with shadow (shadow-xl)
- **Badges**: Small rounded pills with colored backgrounds
- **Inputs**: Border with focus ring effect
- **Messages**: Rounded bubbles (rounded-2xl)
